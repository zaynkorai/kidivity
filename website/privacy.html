Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Kidivity ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience for you and your children. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address via our authentication provider (Supabase). This is used to sync your data across devices.
  • Child Profiles: You may create profiles for your children within the app. We collect minimal information such as a nickname, age group, and educational grade level. We do not require or collect full names or birthdates.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app to improve our activities and features.
  • Device Information: Standard device information such as model and OS version may be collected for diagnostic and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Kidivity service.
  • Personalize activities for your child's age and grade level.
  • Synchronize your data across multiple devices.
  • Improve and optimize our app's performance.
  • Communicate with you regarding account-related matters.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption provided by our backend partner, Supabase. We only share information when necessary to provide our services (e.g., authentication) or when required by law.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kidivity is designed for parents to use with their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All data collection (such as child profile creation) is initiated and managed by the parent or legal guardian. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

5. Your Choices and Data Deletion

You can manage and delete your data at any time within the app's settings menu. Deleting your account will permanently remove all associated parent and child data from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@kidivity.pro.
