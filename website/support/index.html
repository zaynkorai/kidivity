Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account data?
You can delete your account and all associated data within the app by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account.

The app is not generating activities. What should I do?
Please ensure you have an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or checking for updates in the App Store.
