2- App's Purpose:
Kaivity is an educational productivity app designed to generate personalized printable activities for children. The app addresses the problem of excessive screen time and generic worksheets by providing parents and educators dynamically tailored, offline, screen-free learning activities. These activities are customized based on the child's age, difficulty preference, and topic interests, helping kids engage in meaningful foundational learning.

3- Instructions for Accessing and Reviewing:
The app handles authentication automatically and seamlessly via anonymous guest login. It does not require any account registration, email, or password form-filling, meaning NO login credentials or dummy accounts are needed for Apple Review reviewers to explore the app. To review the app's core features:

    a- Launch the app and proceed through the initial onboarding screens.
    b- Create a Kid Profile by selecting an avatar, nick name, and relative age.
    c- Navigate to the "Generate" tab, and select an academic or creative category (e.g., Puzzles, Tracing, Math).
    d- Select a sub-topic, choose your difficulty, select output style (Colorful or B&W), and tap "Generate Activity".
    e- A fully personalized, printable PDF activity will be displayed, which you can save via the native share sheet or send directly to a printer.

4- External Services, Tools, or Platforms:
Kaivity uses a secure custom backend to orchestrate the activity generation. The core functionality relies on:

    a- Supabase: Handles secure, anonymous on-device authentication and securely stores user kid profiles and historical activity metadata.
    b- Backend API: Coordinates the generative workflow and stores final PDFs via cloud storage (Cloudflare R2).
    c- Google Generative AI (via our backend): Dynamically creates and structures the completely unique educational problem sets.

5- Regional Differences:

The app functions identically across all global regions. There are no geoblocked features, IP-restricted functions, or regional content changes at this time. Language generation relies primarily on standard English formatting.

6- Highly Regulated Industry:
This app does not operate in a highly regulated industry. It is a standard educational utility for generating children's paper-based learning materials. No special authorization documentation, medical credentials, or financial disclosures are required for its operation.


7- Does the app include third-party analytics? If so, please provide details about what data is collected for this purpose. 
Answer: No. The app does not include or implement any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Firebase Analytics, etc.) and does not track user behavior across the app.

8- Does the app include third-party advertising? If so, please provide a link to the ad network's publicly-documented practices and policies for kids apps.
Answer: No. The app is completely ad-free. It does not integrate any third-party advertising networks or display any promotional material.

9- Will the data be shared with any third parties? If so, for what purposes and where will this information be stored?

Answer: Data is never shared with third-party data brokers, ad networks, or external analytics providers.

The only external data flow occurs as an inherent part of the app's core functionality. When generating a personalized worksheet, anonymous inputs (such as the chosen topic and relative age) are securely transmitted to our custom backend. Our backend then connects securely via API to Google Generative AI to construct the educational problem sets. The generated PDF activities are temporarily stored in our secure, private cloud bucket (Cloudflare R2) purely so the app can retrieve and display the printable file to the user. All data processed in this workflow is strictly anonymous and cannot be traced to any specific individual.

10- Is the app collecting any user or device data for purposes beyond third-party analytics or third-party advertising? If so, please provide a complete and clear explanation of all planned uses of this data.

Answer: We collect a minimal amount of user-provided information strictly to fulfill the core educational functionality of the app.

To appropriately personalize the difficulty and theme of the learning activities, the user is asked to select a "nickname," an "avatar," and a "relative age group" during onboarding. This data is handled via a secure, anonymous guest session (authorized seamlessly on the device without requiring email or passwords) and is safely stored in our database (Supabase). This information is solely used to generate customized activities and maintain a history track so parents can print past worksheets.

The app completely avoids collecting Personally Identifiable Information (PII), identifiers for advertisers (IDFA), precise location data, or any out-of-bounds device telemetry.
